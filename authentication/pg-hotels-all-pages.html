About This Property

Nestled in the heart of Civil Lines, The Grand Residency is Nagpur's most celebrated luxury address. Spread across three heritage wings, the property seamlessly blends Mughal architecture with contemporary comforts. Each of our 120 rooms and suites has been individually curated by award-winning designers, offering a sanctuary of refined elegance. Our rooftop infinity pool overlooks the city skyline, while Maharaja's Table — our flagship restaurant — serves modern Indian cuisine with Michelin-trained precision.
